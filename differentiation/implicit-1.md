## Implicit Problem 1

_Given..._

* x^2 + y^2 = 25
* dx/dt = 7
* find dy/dt when x = 3

**Step 1: Find the value of y when x = 3**

* 3^2 + y^2 = 25
* 9 + y^2 = 25
* y^2 = 25 - 9
* y = sqrt(16)
* y = 4

**Step 2: Differentiate implicityly with respect to t**

* take derivative of both sides: 
* d/dt(x^2 + y^2) = d/dt(25)
* chain rule:
* 2x(dx/dt) + 2y(dy/dt) = 0

**Step 3: Solve for dy/dt**

* 2y(dy/dt) = -2x(dx/dt)
* dy/dt = -x(dx/dt) / y

**Step 4: Substitute the known values**

* if y = 7: dy/dt = -(3)(7)/4 = -21/4 = -5.25
* if y = -7: dy/dt -(3)(7)/-4 = 21/4 = 5.25
